Section 5 reflective narrative (required, 50–100 words)
Experienced: "Describe a situation where you had to work with someone you found genuinely difficult — a colleague, a client, a manager. What made it difficult, what did you do, and how did it resolve?"

Early career: "Describe a situation where you had to navigate a relationship that felt difficult or uncomfortable. What made it hard and what did you do?"

LLM rubric for Section 5 narrative
Criterion	Dimension and what LLM evaluates
Empathy / perspective-taking	Relational Intelligence: Does the candidate show any curiosity about why the other person behaved as they did?
Outcome orientation	Ownership: Do they focus on resolving the situation or on describing how difficult it was?
Self-awareness	Relational Intelligence: Do they acknowledge their own role in the dynamic?
Communication approach	Communication Confidence: Do they describe direct intentional communication or avoidance?
